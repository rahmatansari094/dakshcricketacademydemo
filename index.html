About Us

The foundation of the Daksh Cricket Academy was laid with the sole aim of providing quality cricket training, world-class cricketing infrastructure, and opportunity to learn from experts in the field of cricket through a fun and innovative environment.


We promise to provide quality training to budding cricket talents world-over. Through our structured coaching programs, regular practice matches, tournaments and interactive sessions with experts, young talents are chiselled to become skilled cricketers.
